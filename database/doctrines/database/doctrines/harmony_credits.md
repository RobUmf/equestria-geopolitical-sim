# The Harmony Credits (H-Credits) Doctrine
**(Version 3.0 - Integrated Mechanics & Asset Governance)**

## Overview
This document outlines the operational guidelines for the **Harmony Credits (H-Credits)** system, a parallel, non-monetary exchange medium tied directly to social, ethical, and infrastructural improvements mandated by the Doctrine of Harmonious Governance[cite: 10, 11]. 

H-Credits act as the primary structural counterweight to standard monetary capital (Bits)[cite: 10]. While Bits dictate raw financial liquidity, H-Credits govern **social leverage and constitutional authority**[cite: 10]. Unchecked capital accumulation cannot unilaterally purchase land or force industrial development without expending H-Credits to secure local and legislative clearance[cite: 10].

---

## 🏛️ The Tripartite Check-and-Balance Matrix

The H-Credit system operates within a closed-loop macroeconomic engine that pits financial capital generation against civic harmony, ensuring no single faction can permanently hijack the geopolitical landscape[cite: 10].
