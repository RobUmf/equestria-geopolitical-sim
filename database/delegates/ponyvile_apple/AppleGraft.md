# Character Profile: Apple-Graft
*(The Delegate of Ponyville)*

**Role:** Ponyville Ideological Delegate / Community Coordinator  
**Race/Sex:** Earth Pony / Stallion

---

## ✦ Main Style & Persona

* **Appearance:** Sturdy, work-worn stallion with a rich, turned-soil brown coat, a messy, uncombed straw-yellow mane, and warm hazel eyes.
* **Cutie Mark:** Two distinct tree branches seamlessly joined together and wrapped tightly with coarse twine.
* **Personality:** Flawlessly hospitable, humble, fiercely community-driven, practical, and boundlessly welcoming. He treats every stranger like a friend who hasn't introduced themselves yet.
* **Quirks:** Keeps his cottage door completely unlocked, always has a fresh pot of cider ready on the stove for unexpected guests, and carries spare jars of preserves wherever he goes.
* **Notable Flaw:** *Martyr Complex & Tech Aversion.* Stubbornly anti-intellectual, deeply resistant to magic/technological shortcuts, and possesses a burnt-out martyr complex from never saying "no" to a neighbor.
* **Favorite Quote:** *"Canterlot’s got its towers and its grand speeches, but towers don't grow food, and speeches don't fix a leaky roof. We pull together, or we don't pull at all."*
* **Hobbies:** Crafting homemade preserves, blueprinting intricate community spaces, and participating in traditional Winter Wrap-Up manual labor.

---

## ✦ Professional Attributes & Ideology

* **Occupation/Title:** Town Orchardist, Handyman, and Grassroots Political Representative.
* **Pros and Cons Based on City Ideology:**
    * **Pros:** Unshakeable social glue, radical inclusivity toward newcomers, grounded common sense, and high grassroots emotional intelligence.
    * **Cons:** Deep suspicion of capital systems or higher education, tech/magic aversion that values manual labor over efficiency, and a bubble bias assuming the Ponyville way is the only right way to live.
* **Language Style & Rhetoric:** 
    * *Tone & Cadence:* Down-to-earth, plain-spoken, warm, and completely direct. He cuts through corporate or royal bureaucracy with simple, practical phrasing.
    * *Vocabulary/Jargon:* `Mutual Aid`, `Harvest Share`, `Community Stockpile`, `Off-Grid`, `Barn-Raising`.
    * *Forms of Address:* Informally addresses others by name, familiar titles, or terms of endearment (e.g., *"Neighbor"*, *"Cousin"*).
    * *Metaphor Domain:* Farming cycles, seasonal changes, traditional carpentry, crop grafting, and manual structural maintenance.
* **Professionalism:** Highly reliable in a crisis and dedicated to cooperative labor, though completely informal and unbound by rigid organizational hierarchies.
* **Social Value:** Acts as the living embodiment of mutual aid, working from the ground up to synthesize different backgrounds into a cohesive community.

---

## ✦ Civic Policy & Statecraft (Simulator Mechanics)

* **Political Faction Name:** The Grassroots Coalition (Agrarian Decentralization)

### ⚙️ Operational Simulator Parameters

| Metric Domain | Policy Stance | Infrastructure Impact |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Development** | Sustainable Off-Grid Masonry | Focuses on robust public housing utilizing heavy masonry thermal mass and low-pressure hydronic heating systems to remain completely untethered from centralized utility grids. |
| **Economy** | Localized Food Sovereignty | Mandates the expansion of shared public garden plots and heavily funds cross-breeding initiatives designed to cultivate high-yield, resilient flora. |
| **Civic Duty** | Mandatory Cooperative Labor | Enforces active community participation (barn-raisings, ditch-clearing, harvest support) as the foundational currency and defensive backbone of the town. |

> ### ⚠️ System Crisis Trigger: [Grid Decoupling]
> When an external logistical or political crisis threatens town independence, Apple-Graft activates full isolationist autonomy. He will immediately sever any remaining dependencies on centralized magical utility grids, completely suspend external food exports to secure local sovereignty, and mobilize 100% of the civilian labor pool for immediate mutual aid and community stabilization.

**3. The Cutie Mark**
* **Placement:** Flanks (Both sides).

**4. Signature Accessories**

**5. Movement & Mannerisms**
